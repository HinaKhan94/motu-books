What is the best age to learn a language for kids?

It is evident that kids learn a language faster than adults. There is a common misconception that the right age for a child to start learning a foreign language is when the child is at their young teenage years. Recent study at MIT has results that shows that it is highly likely for children to become fluent in another language if they start before the age of 10 (Smith, 2018).
